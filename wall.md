# Wall of Lamentations

A colossal relic covered in inscriptions written in a forgotten language, holding the names of those who descended into the Abyss and never returned.

Time has buried them... but the wall **speaks**.

Rumors say that if you make an offering of your blood, you can reveal the wandering soul of the ancestors who fell in the abyss.

The inscriptions light up and flash names for a moment: yours, a companion's, someone who hasn't fallen yet.

The wall doesn't just remember those who came before — **it senses those who are yet to come**.

Make your offering. Carve your name into the stone. Become legend.

---

## Wandering Souls

Those who have survived the Abyss claim that after death, the soul doesn't disappear. It changes. It transforms. It becomes a **Wandering Soul**.

Shadows that drift across the island. Echoes of those who defied the impossible.

Some are faint. Others pulse with force. All of them carry the essence of a past that refuses to die.

Walking among them is feeling the weight of an eternal legacy.

---

### How to get a Wandering Soul

Wandering Souls are symbols of commitment, participation, and individual impact on the birth of the Abyss. Earning one is becoming a living part of the game's legacy.

Current ways to obtain them:

- **Soul Skeleton**
  A ghostly skeleton in celestial blue — ethereal and silent.
  - **How to obtain:** By participating in the *Catalepsy* prototype.
  - **Perceived value:** Represents those who were there from the beginning. Wearing this form means being part of the genesis of the Abyss.

- **Soul Skeleton with Aura**
  A variant of the Soul Skeleton that radiates a distinctive purple aura — a sign of influence and impact.
  - **How to obtain:** By playing the prototype and inviting at least 5 people to the official Discord server.
  - **Perceived value:** Special recognition for helping expand the community in its earliest stages.

- **Ambassador Soul Skeleton**
  An imposing figure with an orange spectral body and a bright aura.
  - **How to obtain:** By leading a community and reaching the TOP 50 in the ambassador rankings.
  - **Perceived value:** Represents a key role in expanding the *Catalepsy* universe. A symbol of leadership and collective reach.
