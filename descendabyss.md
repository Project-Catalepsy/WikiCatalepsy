# Abyssal Descent

The **Abyssal Descent** is the main competitive mode of *Catalepsy*. Players descend through the Infernal Abyss and are evaluated on their performance: speed, efficiency in defeating enemies, and item usage are all key factors in the final score.

---

## Seasons

Competitions are organized in **35-day seasons**, followed by a 15-day rest period — giving players time to **recover, rethink strategies, and prepare** for the next season.

> Each season presents unique challenges through mutations and curses, ensuring every descent is different.

---

### Mutations

- Enemies can receive **mutations** that alter their behavior, changing attacks and movement patterns.
- **48 mutations** have been designed, with **12 chosen at random** each season — forcing players to constantly adapt.

### Curses

- Players, through the **Conclave**, vote to choose **3 curses** that disable certain abilities — making each season a distinct, balanced challenge.

---

## How to compete

- Before entering the Infernal Abyss, activate **Abyssal Descent mode**.
- During the descent, stats such as enemies defeated, speed, and item usage are tracked to determine performance.

## Rewards

Each division rewards the **top players in each section**, based on their accumulated score during the descent:
