# Project: Catalepsy

A 3D roguelike where every step is a deadly threat. No backtracking. No saves. No safety net.

If you've played Hades, Hollow Knight, or Dark Souls, you know what it feels like to lose and try again. *Catalepsy* takes that feeling and drops it into a three-dimensional hell where the environment, the enemies, and your own decisions conspire against you from level one. The difference isn't just the difficulty — it's that here, space is a weapon too.

---

## What is Catalepsy

A third-person roguelike set in a hell inspired by Christian tradition. We've abandoned the genre's typical isometric perspective to deliver a fully **3D** experience, built around three pillars: **fast movement, strategic combat, and a hostile environment**.

You descend through the Abyss across **20 procedurally generated levels**. Every five floors, a boss blocks your path. No mid-run saves. No retreating. Every decision is irreversible.

---

## Core Pillars

### 🏃 Movement as survival
Positioning and spatial awareness matter as much as attacking. Move well, survive longer. Stand still, die.

### 🎯 Pattern-based combat
Enemies aren't obstacles — they're systems you need to understand and master. Skill is built through every death.

### 🌑 Environmental hostility
The environment is not decoration. It's an active threat. Flora, architecture, and darkness work against you.

---

## Universe

Set in the **Infernal Abyss** — a desolate, hostile territory born from the eternal conflict between good and evil. The narrative revolves around descent, atonement, and confrontation with forces that go beyond the human. Dark and oppressive tones, gothic and decadent aesthetics, macabre elements that aren't decoration — they're part of the world.

## What the current prototype includes

**Included:**

- 1 functional playable environment.
- Base melee combat system.
- Full character movement (dodge, repositioning).
- Basic enemies with defined patterns.
- 1 boss-style encounter (vertical slice).
- Initial preparation lobby.
- Original soundtrack.

**Not yet included:**

- The full 20 planned levels.
- The 5 final bosses.
- Advanced progression systems.
- Definitive balancing.
- Expanded narrative content.

## Core Loop

1. Preparation — choose abilities, equip amulets, set your strategy.
2. Descent — 20 levels with no way back.
3. Combat — adapt, improvise, survive.
4. Boss defeat — rewards and a decision: push further or retreat.
5. Death or retreat — the Gate keeps your progression. Everything else is gone.
6. New descent — with more knowledge, better preparation, against harder odds.

---

Catalepsy isn't trying to be just another roguelike.
It's about bringing the tension of descent into three dimensions — and making every floor an irreversible decision.
